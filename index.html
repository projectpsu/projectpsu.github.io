Penn State Interactive Scholarship Management

This page is designed to see how Penn State football scholarships are distributed across classes for now and the future.

Drag and drop players to change position or eligibility. Click players to change status. See possible statuses below.

Status Options:

Player Name
  This is a player on the roster who has already used their redshirt year previously and will not redshirt this year. Also used to represent an incoming recruit.

Player Name
  This is a player who is not redshirting this year, but has the redshirt available if needed. Only available for players currently on the roster.

Player Name
  This is a player who is redshirting (or expected to) during the current season. Only available for players currently on the roster

Player Name
  This is an incoming recruit who is expected to grayshirt. Only an available option for incoming recruits.

Player Name
  This is a player or recruit who is expected to leave sometime before the next season, whether due to transfer, graduation, entering the NFL draft early, decommit, etc. Available for all players and incoming recruits.

Advance Season: Moves all scholarships to the next season. Players who are redshirting do not lose a year of eligibility, but lose the option to RS next year. Players not redshirting with only 1 year of eligibility remaining are removed from the list. Players whose status is leaving are removed from the list. Incoming players are listed with four years of eligibility and using their redshirt. Grayshirt incoming recruits stay in the Incoming list. Everyone else is moved ahead, losing a year of eligibility.
