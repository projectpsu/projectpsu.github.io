This page is designed to see how Penn State football scholarships are distributed across classes now and in the future.

General use is to drag and drop players to change position or eligibility. Click players to change status. Click the target values to change them. See possible statuses below. The other menu buttons are also explained below.

Player Status

Player Name
  This is a player on the roster who has already used their redshirt year previously and will not redshirt this year. Also used to represent an incoming recruit.

Player Name
  This is a player who is not redshirting this year, but has the redshirt available if needed. Only available for players currently on the roster.

Player Name
  This is a player who is redshirting (or expected to) during the current season. Only available for players currently on the roster.

Player Name
  This is an incoming recruit who is expected to grayshirt. Only an available option for incoming recruits.

Player Name
  This is a player or recruit who is expected to leave sometime before the next season for whatever reason. Available for all players and incoming recruits.

Advance Season

Moves all scholarships to the next season. Players who are redshirting do not lose a year of eligibility, but lose the option to RS next year. Players not redshirting with only 1 year of eligibility remaining are removed from the list. Players whose status is leaving are removed from the list. Incoming players are listed with four years of eligibility and using their redshirt. Grayshirt incoming recruits stay in the Incoming list. Everyone else is moved ahead, losing a year of eligibility.

Add Recruits

Click this to bring up the areas to add recruits. You can add recruits at any position. You can also move them onto the roster (e.g. for someone who transfers in). Click the blue box to enter a name. Enter key or clicking elsewhere will add the player, while Escape key cancels.

Filter

Opens a sub-menu. Filter players to make it easier to see who is on offense, defense, or ST's. Also can identify who is redshirting, who has a redshirt available, who has used their redshirt already, and who is leaving.

File Management

Opens a sub-menu. Allows you to create a "save" file (.json) of the current roster state. Can also upload a previously saved file. To save a file, click "Create File" in the sub-menu, then the "Download File" link will appear with the file to download.
